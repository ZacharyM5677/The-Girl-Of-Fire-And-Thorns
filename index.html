I got married today to King Alejandro of Joya d’Arena. It's my 16th birthday. I
barely know him, and my dress feels like it’s squeezing the life
out of me. Everyone keeps saying I’m chosen by God, but honestly
I feel nervous, and not important at all. I really hope
I don’t embarrass my whole kingdom. I have a Godstone in my navel,
which signals I'm truly chosen by god. It's useless to me, though;
It does nothing but tingle with a warm sensation when I prey.

This desert never ends. It’s just pure heat. Alejandro barely
talks to me, I just wish this was over. I have to travel to a desert
country called Joya d'Arena which was about to be at war with the country
of Invierne. I don't want to fight a war, I want to know what this gem
in my navel is meant to be for.

They showed up fast. Loud drums, fire everywhere, horses screaming.
The Invierne. I tried to help Aneaxi, my lady-in-waiting, when the carriage flipped, but
her leg broke. She didn’t scream, but she looked like she was in pain.
I just froze. I couldn’t move or do anything helpful. What’s the point
of this Godstone if I can’t even protect someone?

After we arrived to Joya d'Arena, Aneaxi died. She never
told us how bad her wound really was. She didn’t want to make
things harder for us, but her wound got infected; The infection
spread and killed her. I feel like it’s my fault, I wasn’t
careful enough. I’ll never forget her.

This morning I woke up in a carriage, but I wasn't able to move.
Turns out a friend from Joya d'Arena, Cosme, and her brother, Humberto,
put me in a carriage to go to a desert village. I couldnt move because they
used duerma leafs, they slow or stop movement entierly. I guess they used
it to keep me still. It's unsettling, though.

We made it to the village. They were really unorgianized and were in
desparate need of a leader. After staying for a couple days, they
had a new leader; me. We all formed a group called the Malficio.
I never thought I would ever be suited as a leader.

Cosme, Humberto, and I all were traveling back, and took a pitstop
at a cave. My Godstone had gone cold, signaling the Inviernes arrival.
I have no idea how they found us, but they captured us. I thought it
would be over, but at least they don't know I have a Godstone.

They brang me to a sorcerer. They tried to mind control me with an
amulet with the Godstone of the previous Godstone bearer from a hundred
years ago. The Godstone should've been dead because its bearer was dead,
But somehow the amulet reactivated it. Their attempt to mind control me
failed, as I was a godstone bearer. I pretended to fall under the control
to avoid suspicion. I was taken to a tent alone with only me and the sorcerer.
He stepped out for a second after placing wine on the table. I noticed duerma
berries on the table, Duerma berries are known for their ability to put people asleep.
I crushed some into his drink while he was gone. He came back and drank some,
and soon fell asleep. I used this opprotunity to put on his cloak,
steal his amulet, and burn the tent with his candle. By putting on
his cloak, I was able to disguise as him and leave the camp without any suspicion.
Luckily, I met up with Cosme and Humberto not too long after.

As we were traveling, the father of Alejandro's mistress, Conde Trevino, sent a letter to us,
wanting to ally with us. Me, Cosme, and Humberto decided to go meet up with him.
Worst mistake ever. Once we got there, he captured us. Turns out Trevino was allied with the Invierne.
He took Humberto and I to a room where Trevino asked me to disclose the location of
the village where the Malficio was located. I refused. Trevino then killed Humberto
after Humberto lied of being the leader of Malficio and to not hurt me. Right as he was
about to die, he spoke of his love for me. After he died, Me and the rest managed to escape
back to the capital of Joya d'Arena. On the way back, I couldn't stop thinking about Humbertos
death.

After making it back to the capital, Alejandro barely recognised me. I
guess that shows how much I've grown, huh? I didn't have much time to
celebrate though, as the Invierne launched a violent attack against the capital.
Many were killed or hurt badly, so I needed to do something fast. I decided
to use the amulet I had gotten from my time at the Inviernes camp and
use its power along with my Godstone. The power of my Godstone and the amulet
combined created a huge magical blast which killed or hurt many of the Invierne.
That move alone made them retreat. Alejandro was deeply injured from the
attack, and would die very soon. Before he died, he trusted me to be
Queen of Joya d'Arena. I shall bring this country to a new age!



Summary


The Girl of Fire and Thorns is about a girl named Lucero-Elisa,
a princess from Orovalle who is chosen by God and carries a
powerful gem called the Godstone in her navel. At the
start of the story, she doesn't feel confident in herself. She thinks
she's too chubby, short, and the opposite of brave. On her 16th birthday,
she’s forced to marry a king named Alejandro from a desert
kingdom called Joya d'Arena. She doesn’t know him, and she’s nervous
due to her insucurities.

As Elisa travels to her new home, their group is attacked by the
Invierne, an enemy nation with dead Godstones which are being
used for their power via special amulets. During the attack, one
of Elisa’s closest companions, Aneaxi, gets badly hurt. Later, Aneaxi dies
because her injury becomes infected, and Elisa feels guilty for not noticing
the injury sooner. This is one of the first moments that shows Elisa starting
to care for others and take responsibility.

Later in the story, Elisa gets taken away by some of her friends, and
finds a village away from the capital of Joya d'Arena. ends up becoming a leader of a rebel group called the Malficio. She helps poor or damaged villages and stands up to
the Invierne. She gets kidnapped by a powerful Invierne sorcerer but tricks
him and escapes by using duerma berries (a sleeping drug) and some
clever thinking after he falls asleep from the berries. She steals a Godstone amulet from the
sorcerer, which helps her later.

Eventually, Elisa loses someone she cares about, Humberto, who might have
loved her. She realizes she doesn’t really love King Alejandro, but still
chose to protect his people. She returns to the capital of Joya d'Arena.
Alejandro almost didn't recognize Elisa, showing how much Elisa has evolved
over the story. As a final attempt, the Invierne invate the capital in attempt
to kill Elisa for her Godstone once in for all. In the final battle, Elisa uses the
Godstone and the amulet she stole to defeat the Invierne army.
Alejandro dies from injuries, and Elisa becomes the Queen of Joya d'Arena.
She has grown into a strong and brave leader.

For my project, I made a social media profile that shows how Elisa
changes throughout the book. In her early posts, I tried to make her sound
insecure about herself, just like she is in the beginning
of the book. As the posts go on, she becomes more confident.
I made sure to add posts about important events, like the caravan
attack, Aneaxi’s death, and her time with the Malficio. I also
showed her relationships with people like Ximena, Humberto, and King Alejandro.
